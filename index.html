About Me

I’m a creative freelancer and multimedia artist helping small businesses elevate their visual presence through design, photography, and branding. My work blends digital precision with handcrafted detail, creating visuals that feel both intentional and personal. Across everything I make - whether for clients or myself - I aim to create pieces that feel thoughtful, expressive, and memorable.
