About emmjoelle

Artist • Designer • Storyteller

Hello! I'm Emma 👋

I’m a multidisciplinary artist and designer passionate about visual storytelling — from photography and branding to ceramics, embroidery, and wearable art.

My work explores the relationship between creativity and identity, combining digital and tangible mediums to express emotion, texture, and narrative.

I love building things that make people pause, smile, or see something differently — whether that’s through a logo, a handmade object, or a photograph.
