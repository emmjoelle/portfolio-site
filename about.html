About emmjoelle

Artist • Designer • Storyteller

Hello! I'm Emma 👋

I’m a local creative freelancer and multimedia specialist who helps small businesses elevate their visual presence through logo design, website updates, photography, and marketing materials. My work blends digital precision with handcrafted detail, creating visuals that feel both intentional and personal to each brand.

Alongside my professional work, I’m a multidisciplinary artist exploring creativity through a range of mediums- from ceramics and embroidery to watercolor and digital painting.

At the core of everything I create - whether it’s a logo, a photograph, or a handmade piece - is the goal to make people pause, feel something, or see things a little differently.
